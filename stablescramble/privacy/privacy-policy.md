# Privacy Policy — Stable Scramble

**Last updated: April 8, 2026**

MaiGuide ("we", "us", or "our") operates the mobile game **Stable Scramble** (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information We Do Not Collect

Stable Scramble does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information directly.

### Information Collected Automatically

**Local game data.** Your high score and audio mute preference are saved locally on your device using the device's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

**Advertising data (via Google AdMob).** The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve relevant ads, AdMob may collect:

- Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
- IP address (used to estimate general location at country/region level)
- App interaction data (e.g., ad impressions and clicks)
- Device information (e.g., device model, OS version, language)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not have direct access to this data.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We do not use your personal data for any purpose because we do not collect it.

The advertising data collected by Google AdMob is used to:

- Display advertisements within the App
- Measure ad performance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations

---

## 3. Advertising and Your Choices

The App uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads and app open ads. Ads may be targeted based on your interests using the Android Advertising ID.

**You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time:**

- On Android: go to **Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID** (Android 12+) or **Opt out of Ads Personalization** (earlier versions).

For more information about how Google uses advertising data, visit [Google's Advertising Policies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party.

The only third-party service integrated into the App is **Google AdMob**, which operates under its own privacy policy as noted above. No other analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs are included in the App.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Stable Scramble is a general-audience arcade game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not include chat features, social functionality, or any mechanism for users to submit personal information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to remove that information.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. High score and preference data stored locally on your device is retained until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.

---

## 7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the App from unauthorized access. Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, the attack surface for a data breach is minimal. The only data at risk is advertising identifiers managed by Google on our behalf, which are protected by Google's security practices.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of those changes.
